Privacy Policy for EasyMeal App

At EasyMeal, we value the privacy of our users and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect from our users and how we use and protect that information.

Collection of Information

When you create an account on EasyMeal, we collect your name, email address, and a password. We may also collect other information that you provide to us voluntarily, such as your dietary preferences or any recipe collections you create.

We may also automatically collect certain information about your device when you use our app, including your IP address, browser type, and operating system.

Use of Information

We use the information we collect from you to provide you with personalized content and recommendations, and to improve our app and services. We may also use your information to communicate with you about important updates or changes to our app.

Sharing of Information

We will not share your personal information with any third parties without your consent, except as required by law or to enforce our own rights or property.

Security of Information

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect from our users, including encryption and other security measures. However, we cannot guarantee that our security measures will prevent unauthorized access or use of your personal information.

Your Choices

You may choose to delete your EasyMeal account at any time. If you do so, we will delete your personal information from our servers within a reasonable amount of time.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our app or services. We will notify you of any material changes to this policy by email or by posting a notice on our website.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@easymeal.com.
